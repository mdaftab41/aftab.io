The Tomb of Sher Shah Suri is in Sasaram in Rohtas district of Bihar. The construction work of this tomb was started by Sher Shah himself and completed by his son and successor Islam Shah some 3 months after the death of Sher Shah in 1545 A.D

Nalanda University was one of the world's first residential universities, as it had dormitories for students.It is also one of the most famous universities. In its heyday it accommodated over 10,000 students and 2,000 teachers.The university was considered an architectural masterpiece, and was marked by a lofty wall and one gate.Nalanda had eight separate compounds and ten temples, along with many meditation halls and classrooms.

Gol Ghar is a round house built on the banks of River Ganga in Patna, Bihar. Originally designed as a storehouse, this structure was built in the year 1786 by Captain John Garstin.the Gol Ghar provides the visitors with panoramic views of the River Ganga.
